AMP-high Cell Sub-clustering

18,651 cells from AMP-high clusters (C0, C2, C7, C10, C14) re-clustered into 14 sub-populations.

SC9: Immune Burst Sub-population

361 cells (2% of AMP-high) with AMP score = 1.18 (10x higher than other sub-clusters). Toll pathway fully activated: cact, dl (Dorsal/NF-κB), Bomanin effectors (BomBc1, BomS1).

Key finding: SC9's AMP profile matches bulk vFB (r = 0.394, all vFB-like), confirming these cells represent the immune effector state of fat body. However, Toll pathway genes (Dif, dl, cact) are uniquely high in SC9 but NOT enriched in bulk vFB vs SAT — indicating SC9 is a rare immune burst state whose signal is diluted in bulk data.
