Quick:
AMP-high Sub-cluster Annotation

14 sub-clusters resolved by re-running PCA → Harmony → UMAP → Leiden on the 18,651 AMP-high cells (original clusters C0, C2, C7, C10, C14). No published study has resolved adult Drosophila fat body immune-active cells into subtypes by scRNA-seq — FCA (Li et al. 2022) and AFCA (Lu et al. 2023) both classified fat body as a single cell type.

Re-clustering recipe
Input: 18,651 cells from AMP-high clusters C0, C2, C7, C10, C14 of the integrated FCA + AFCA fat body atlas.
Pipeline: raw log-normalised counts → 3,000 highly variable genes (seurat v1) → scale (clip 10) → PCA (30 PCs) → Harmony batch correction on dataset (FCA/AFCA, converged in 28 iter) → neighbours (k = 20 on X_pca_harmony) → UMAP (min_dist = 0.3, seed 42) → Leiden (resolution = 0.6, seed 42) → 14 sub-clusters.
Markers: Wilcoxon rank_genes_groups over the sub-Leiden labels on the raw-count matrix.
